About Us

KAHANI BUFFET is a premier entertainment platform offering a unique glimpse into the lives and minds of renowned personalities from India and worldwide. Through our curated content, including stories, films, and documentaries, you'll gain insights into their thoughts, lifestyles, and opinions on their audience.

Our Journey

Since launching in 2025, we've been working diligently to fulfill our vision. Our upcoming schedule includes podcasts with renowned Bollywood personalities, followed by films, documentaries, and interviews planned through March 2026.

Our Vision

Our mission is to bring you closer to Bollywood by sharing personal stories of its stars. We feature films showcasing artistic brilliance, soulful music, political insights, and athlete wellness tips.
